About Me

I am a PhD student at Dalhousie University with expertise in stream processing, energy profiling, and machine learning. My research focuses on sustainable AI development and driving innovation in large language model integrated stream processing applications. I work under the supervision of Dr. Israat Haque and mentorship of Dr. Miguel Neves, with an expected graduation in December 2025.
